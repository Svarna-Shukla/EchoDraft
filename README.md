EchoDraft BrainDump:

So EchoDraft is a founder's dream partner come true. EchoDraft has mainly a bunch of features that benefit young founders or even enthusiasts who want to validate a random idea they got at 3 am while scrolling through Instagram.

Features list that I want to implement:

1. Live Deck Build: This is for founders on the go. Suppose you have an idea you want to pitch and you have a meeting about it in the next 2 minutes. You basically have a complete brain map of that idea. Open Live Deck and brain dump your entire idea. By the time you finish, you will already be able to see the entire slide deck. Backed by real data and not just made up stats.

2. Flowchart: After brain dumping the idea, click a button called "FlowChart" to instantly create a complete flowchart of the idea including every aspect branching into another.

3. Competitor research: The feature instantly looks up and searches for data in real time and searches for real competitors for the entrepreneur's idea. 

*** Hero Feature: The app automatically creates a card for the competitor company. The card has these features:
    - Revenue
    - Customer Composition
    - Competitor's USP
    - End User: (B2B, B2C, etc.)
    - Resemblance to ur prodcut (in %)
    - Years in the market
    - Marketing Strategy
    - Company Page URL
    - Overall strength rating (Out of 100)

Then, after you provide your idea details, the app creates a card similar to that for your idea. 

4. GTM Strategy: This feature listens to the idea and creates a complete go to market strategy for the entrepreneur.

5. One Pager: After listening to the idea, this feature instantly turns it into a One Pager document.
